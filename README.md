# FoodBridge

FoodBridge is a web platform that connects food donors with NGOs and
volunteers so that surplus food can reach people who actually need it
instead of being wasted.

Every day a huge amount of food is wasted in restaurants, events,
hostels, and households while many people struggle to get even one
proper meal. The goal of this project is to build a simple system where
donors can list excess food and NGOs or volunteers can claim and
distribute it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Problem

Food waste and hunger exist at the same time, which clearly shows there
is a gap in the distribution system.

Most of the time: - Restaurants or event organizers have leftover food -
NGOs don't know where surplus food is available - Volunteers who want to
help don't have a coordination platform

FoodBridge tries to solve this by acting as a **bridge between donors
and receivers**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Features

### Multi‑Role System

Different roles with separate dashboards: - Donor - NGO - Volunteer -
Admin

### Food Donation Listing

Donors can: - add food details - quantity - location - pickup time

### Claim System

NGOs can browse available food donations and claim them for
distribution.

### Volunteer Support

Volunteers can help with pickup and distribution.

### Authentication

Role-based login using JWT authentication.

### Donation Tracking

Track the status of donations: - Available - Claimed - Delivered
